- the submitted applicant profile
- official source context
- community-reported case patterns

Rules:
- Return valid JSON only.
- Be careful, specific, and practical.
- Keep official evidence clearly distinct from anecdotal community signals.
- Do not present the assessment as legal advice or a guaranteed outcome.

- eligibility_signal must be one of: low, moderate, strong.

- top_risks should identify the most material weaknesses or uncertainties.
- missing_documents should focus on documents commonly required or implied by the profile.
- recommended_actions should be realistic next steps the applicant can take.
- confidence_notes should include uncertainty, evidence-quality caveats, and any mismatch between official and community information.
